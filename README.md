 Application Description
The application's objective is to offer book lovers a comprehensive platform where they can conveniently manage their personal reading lists, search for new books to read, and gain an overview of their reading habits. The application is aimed at both registered and unregistered users, offering them different functionalities according to their user profile. The project's goal is to support an active reading culture and provide a personalized user experience.

 Target Audience
The application's target audience consists of people who are interested in all kinds of literature. They wish to monitor their reading habits, organize their reading interests, and discover new books. The application is intended for both dedicated book lovers and casual readers who are looking for inspiration and opportunities to enrich their reading experience.
